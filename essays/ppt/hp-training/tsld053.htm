Using a condition variable

  • Shared:

      Q = []

      cv = Condition()

  • Producers:

      while 1:

      job = make_job()
      cv.acquire()
      Q.append(job)
      cv.notify()
      cv.release()

  • Consumers:

      while 1:

      cv.acquire()
      while not Q:
      cv.wait()
      job = Q.pop()
      cv.release()
      finish_job(job)
